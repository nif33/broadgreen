Welcome to Broadgreen Acupuncture Center!

At Broadgreen Acupuncture Center, we are dedicated to providing top-quality acupuncture and holistic healthcare services to help you achieve optimal well-being. Our center is led by a team of experienced and highly skilled practitioners who are passionate about improving your health and enhancing your quality of life.
In addition to acupuncture, registert massage therapy, we also offer complementary therapies such as cupping, herbal medicine, and moxibustion. These therapies work in harmony with acupuncture to further promote healing and balance within the body. Our practitioners will assess your condition and recommend the most appropriate combination of therapies to optimize your results.

Our Team

Dr. Henry Yang

Dr. TCM

Dr. Henry Yang entered Shandong University of Traditional Chinese Medicine in 1977. Due to his excellent academic performance, he stayed at the university to teach after graduation. In 1994, Dr. Yang came to Vancouver and successively served as a senior lecturer and internship instructor in various disciplines at VCC, Langara College, Vancouver College of Traditional Chinese Medicine, and Vancouver Beijing College of Traditional Chinese Medicine. During the same period, he began practicing medicine in Vancouver in 1995. He is currently a registered senior TCM physician in BC.

Larry Lai

Registered TCM Physicians
Registered Acupuncturists

Coming from a three-generation TCM family, deeply inheriting the true teachings of his grandfather and father, and with a strong passion for TCM since childhood, Lai Jinli entered Fujian Medical University in 1988, majoring in Clinical Medicine. After graduation, he worked in the departments of Gastroenterology, Respiratory Medicine, and Neurology for 20 years. Driven by his love for TCM, Mr. Lai once again entered Fujian University of Traditional Chinese Medicine to study the integrated Chinese and Western medicine program, and furthered his studies in the acupuncture program at Hubei University of Chinese Medicine, obtaining a bachelor's degree.

Hong Ji

Registered Acupuncturists

Mrs. Ji graduated from Tianjin University of Traditional Chinese Medicine in 1992, and studied under the renowned National Master of Chinese Medicine, Academician Shi Xuemin. After graduation, she worked in the Acupuncture Department, Emergency Department, and Inpatient Department of Tianjin Heping Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital for over 30 years, accumulating rich clinical experience. She is skilled in acupuncture and Tuina massage, emphasizing holistic conditioning and providing personalized treatment plans.

Sheldon Tan

Registered Massage Therapist

Sheldon, a graduate of Vancouver Career College, is a professional massage therapist dedicated to helping clients reduce stress, relieve pain, and improve physical function through massage therapy techniques. He strives to provide excellent therapeutic services in the areas of client rehabilitation, sports injury care, and stress management. He specializes in Swedish massage, deep tissue massage, and relaxation massage, and is experienced in treating repetitive strain injuries from long-term work, motor vehicle accidents, prenatal/postnatal care, headaches, acute and chronic back, neck and shoulder pain, sports injuries, and physical or life stress problems.
